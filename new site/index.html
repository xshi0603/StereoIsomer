About Cookie Clicker

This is an addicting game about building your very own cookie generating kingdom. To get a taste of how addicting it is, try clicking the cookie on the right. You start out only being able to get cookies by clicking the big cookie, but soon, you can buy auto clickers, grandmas, and even farms to create cookies for you while you lay back and watch your cookie wealth grow. Here are some features in our version:

  • 3 auto generators: cursors, grandmas, and farms
  • The ability to save and pick up where you left off
  • Upgrades so you can satiate your hunger for cookies quicker
  • A global leaderboard so you can see where you stand in cookie land
